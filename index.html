How do I begin using Growth for my startup

Start by clicking the "Get Started" button on the homepage to sign up for the free Simple plan, which requires no credit card. Follow the easy setup process to start organizing and tracking your startup's progress.

Do I need any technical skills to use Growth?

No technical skills are needed. The platform is designed for ease of use, and the setup can be completed in an afternoon, as shared by other startups in the success stories.

Can I schedule a demo to see Growth in action?

Yes, click the "Book a Demo" button on the homepage to schedule a personalized demo and learn how Growth can benefit your startup.

How do I join a team call to get started?

Look for the team call invitation on the homepage, like the one scheduled for Jan 17 at 9 AM. Click "Join" to participate and get hands-on guidance.

What should I do after signing up for the Simple plan?

After signing up, explore the real-time monitoring and task organization features. Invite your team to collaborate and start tracking key performance indicators right away.

What are the features of the free Simple plan?

The Simple plan includes real-time monitoring, tracking key performance indicators, scheduling appointments, and tools to organize, delegate, and keep track of tasks, all free with no credit card required.

How much does the Premium plan cost?

The Premium plan is priced at $48 per month, offering advanced features to enhance your startup’s growth.

What additional features does the Premium plan offer?

The Premium plan includes everything from the free plan, plus data-driven decisions, data visualization, storing and organizing contact information, and real-time communication tools for messaging and video conferencing.

Is there a trial period for the Premium plan?

While there’s no specific trial for Premium, you can start with the free Simple plan to test the core features. Booking a demo can also help you evaluate the Premium benefits before upgrading.

Are there any hidden costs with Growth’s plans?

No, both the Simple and Premium plans are transparent with no hidden costs. The free plan requires no credit card, and the Premium plan is a flat $48/month with all features included.
